About us

We are a startup company with promising growth potential. Initially, our client base is limited, but we are focused and determined to expand using the potential of mobile technologies. Our current mission as a company is to provide an easy way to assist students who face challenges in their studies, enabling them to level up and meet the expectations of their education. We will develop software that supports students in finding teachers or mentors in the subjects they aim to learn and improve. Similarly, our vision is to become a significant choice in helping students.
